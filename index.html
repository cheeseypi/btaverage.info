Special Rules for Autumn '22

  • Each player will have an extra 'opportunity' to use during their own redemption round.
  • These 'opportunities' can be used only once per person per division (If a player moves on to another division, they can take advantage of that division's opportunity)
  • Blue 💙 and Red ❤️ divisions: Any A-Side, while collecting at least 1/5th of the red berries
  • Yellow 💛, Cracked 🤍, and Lunar 🖤 divisions: Any A-Side, while collecting at least 1/3rd of the red berries
  • The selection of A-Sides with berries counts as a separate level from normal A-Sides, for the purposes of excluding levels (i.e. 5A & 5A with berries may both be chosen in a single division)
